Roadway — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DP Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Roadway mobile application (the "App").

1) Information we collect

a) Collected during trip recording

  • GPS location (latitude, longitude, heading, speed) — recorded only while a trip is actively running.
  • Device motion data (accelerometer readings) — used to detect road surface impacts.
  • Device information (device model, operating system version).
  • Device identifier — a randomly generated UUID created on first launch. This is not linked to your Apple ID, name, email, or any personal account.

b) What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or Apple ID.
  • We do not collect contacts, photos, health data, or browsing history.
  • We do not track your location when the app is not actively recording a trip.
  • We do not require account creation or login.

2) How we use information

  • Detect and classify road hazards (potholes and speed bumps) in real time using motion sensor analysis.
  • Map road conditions by tagging each detection with GPS coordinates, severity, and speed.
  • Aggregate anonymous road condition data on the Roadway Dashboard to identify problem areas and support road improvement efforts.
  • Improve detection accuracy by analyzing aggregate sensor patterns.

We do not sell your data or share it for advertising purposes.

3) Data storage

  • On-device: Trip data and detected events are stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud: Trip and detection data is transmitted to our secure cloud database (Google Firebase/Firestore) for aggregation on the Roadway Dashboard. All data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • No personal identifiers: Cloud-stored data contains only the random device UUID, device model, location coordinates, timestamps, and sensor readings. No personally identifiable information is stored.

4) Sharing

  • Municipal transportation departments: Aggregated, anonymous road condition data may be shared with government agencies to support road repair and infrastructure improvement.
  • Service providers: We use Firebase (Google) to store and process trip data.
  • Legal and safety: We may disclose data where required by law or to protect rights and safety.

We do not share data with third parties for marketing or advertising.

5) Data retention

Trip and detection data is retained to build a comprehensive picture of road conditions over time. You may delete individual trips from your device at any time using the Trips tab in the app. To request deletion of your cloud-stored data, contact us using the information below.

6) Your choices

  • Stop recording: Tap Stop at any time to end a trip. Location and motion data are only collected during active recording.
  • Delete trip data: Remove trips from your device through the app's Trips tab.
  • Revoke permissions: Revoke location or motion permissions at any time through your device's Settings app. The app will not function without these permissions.
  • Request cloud deletion: Contact us to request deletion of your data from our cloud database.

7) Children

The App is rated 4+ and does not collect personally identifiable information. No account creation is required. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data, including Firebase Security Rules and encrypted transport (HTTPS). No system is 100% secure.

9) International transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than yours (e.g., United States via Google Firebase). We use appropriate safeguards, where required, for cross-border transfers.

10) Changes

We may update this Policy. We will post the new version here and update the effective date. Material changes may be communicated by additional notice.
