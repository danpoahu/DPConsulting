LDAH — Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how DP Consulting ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use the LDAH (Leadership in Disabilities and Achievement of Hawaii) mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The LDAH app is a web wrapper that provides convenient mobile access to the LDAH website. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information through the app itself. The app does not require account creation or login.

2. Website Content

The app displays content from the LDAH website (ldahawaii.org). When you browse content through the app, your interaction is subject to the LDAH website's own privacy practices. We encourage you to review the LDAH website's privacy policy for details about how they handle data.

3. No Tracking or Advertising

The app does not use advertising IDs, analytics trackers, or third-party advertising services. We do not sell or share any user data with third parties.

4. Data Storage

The app may store standard web cache data locally on your device to improve performance. This data can be cleared by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the effective date above when changes are made.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: info@dpconsult.app
